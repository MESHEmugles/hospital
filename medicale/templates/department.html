Dental

Our excellent service, friendly team and state-of-the-art facility ensure you will receive the high quality, personalized Dental care

DENTAL & ORAL


At The MEDICAL Hospital's Department of Dentistry, our dentists provide comprehensive care to meet each patient's oral health needs. Treatments cover the full spectrum of dental needs for adults, children, and special needs patients. Our expertise encompass all phases of general dentistry, such as restorative, cosmetic, endodontic, periodontic, fixed and removable prosthetic, and implant services, as well as treatment for temporomandibular joint (TMJ) dysfunction disorders.
Some of the most common treatments we provide include:
  • Veneers
  • Teeth Whitening
  • Dentures
  • Dental Implants
  • Extraction of Impacted Teeth / Wisdom Tooth Extraction
